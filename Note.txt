Các loại Popover:
- My Liked Playlist và My Liked Episodes Thì không có popover. Hiển thị mặc định 
không thể xoá
- Song ->  - Add to playlist
           - Save to your Liked Song   
           - Add to Queue
           - Share
           - Go to Album
- Podcast -> - Add to playlist
           - Save to your Liked Episodes    
           - Add to Queue
           - Share
- Playlist không thể thêm vào Liked Song, nó được tính là Playlist riêng lẻ

- Playlist Song / Album / Episodes
        -> - Add to Library
           - Add to Queue
           - Share

- Playlist Song / Episode (Trong Library - Của mình)
        -> - Share
           - Delete
           - Add to Queue
           - Make Private / Public


- Playlist Song / Episode (Trong Library - Của Artist)
        -> - Share
           - Remove from your Library
           - Add to Queue

- Queue -> - Remove from queue   
           - Save to your liked song/episodes
           - Share
           - Add to Playlist
